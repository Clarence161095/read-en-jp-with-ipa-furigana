PARTpɑrt IItu — CHAPTERˈʧæptər XIɪˈlɛvən

OLDoʊld BARBERINbɑrbəˈræ̃

Ifɪf Iaɪ hadhæd notnɑt beenbɪn inɪn aə hurryˈhɜri totu reachriʧ Parisˈpærɪs, Iaɪ wouldwʊd havehæv stayedsteɪd aə longlɔŋ, longlɔŋ timetaɪm withwɪð Liseliz.

Wewi hadhæd sosoʊ muchmʌʧ totu telltɛl eachiʧ otherˈʌðər, butbʌt ouraʊər languageˈlæŋgwɪʤ ofʌv gesturesˈʤɛsʧərz allowedəˈlaʊd usʌs totu sayseɪ sosoʊ littleˈlɪtəl.

Liseliz toldtoʊld memi aboutəˈbaʊt herhɜr lifelaɪf atæt Decizedəˈsiz withwɪð herhɜr auntænt andænd uncleˈʌŋkəl. Herhɜr auntænt hadhæd bornebɔrn fivefaɪv childrenˈʧɪldrən butbʌt nonenʌn hadhæd survivedsərˈvaɪvd.

Iaɪ toldtoʊld herhɜr howhaʊ Iaɪ nearlyˈnɪrli dieddaɪd inɪn theðə minemaɪn wherewɛr Alexisəˈlɛksɪs workedwɜrkt, andænd thatðæt mymaɪ familyˈfæməli waswʌz searchingˈsɜrʧɪŋ forfɔr memi.

Ofʌv coursekɔrs Iaɪ spokespoʊk mostmoʊst aboutəˈbaʊt mymaɪ wealthyˈwɛlθi familyˈfæməli, theðə prospectˈprɑspɛkt thatðæt, ifɪf realizedˈriəˌlaɪzd, wouldwʊd makemeɪk everyoneˈɛvriˌwʌn happyˈhæpi.

Besidesbɪˈsaɪdz ouraʊər conversationsˌkɑnvərˈseɪʃənz, wewi wentwɛnt onɑn walkswɔks. Fivefaɪv ofʌv usʌs, notnɑt threeθri — forfɔr Mr.ˈmɪstər Capiˈkɑpi andænd Missmɪs Dolldɑl werewɜr alwaysˈɔlweɪz presentˈprɛzənt.

Vastvæst forestsˈfɔrɪsts, meadowsˈmɛdoʊz stretchingˈstrɛʧɪŋ totu theðə horizonhəˈraɪzən, waterfallsˈwɔtərˌfɔlz, stillstɪl pondspɑndz, andænd theðə canalkəˈnæl windingˈwaɪndɪŋ gentlyˈʤɛntli betweenbɪˈtwin theðə hillshɪlz.

Mymaɪ lastlæst wordswɜrdz totu Liseliz beforebɪˈfɔr partingˈpɑrtɪŋ: "Iaɪ shallʃæl comekʌm forfɔr youju inɪn aə coachkoʊʧ drawndrɔn bybaɪ fourfɔr horsesˈhɔrsɪz!"

Andænd Liseliz believedbɪˈlivd memi sosoʊ completelykəmˈplitli thatðæt sheʃi wavedweɪvd herhɜr handhænd asæz ifɪf crackingˈkrækɪŋ aə whipwɪp. Sheʃi couldkʊd seesi theðə coachkoʊʧ, justʤʌst asæz Iaɪ couldkʊd.

Mattiamɑˈtiɑ insistedɪnˈsɪstɪd wewi keepkip earningˈɜrnɪŋ moneyˈmʌni alongəˈlɔŋ theðə wayweɪ. Hehi saidsɛd: "Wewi can'tkænt bebi sureʃʊr we'llwil findfaɪnd oldoʊld Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃ rightraɪt awayəˈweɪ!"

Inɪn Parisˈpærɪs, wewi partedˈpɑrtɪd atæt Placeplɑs d'Italiediˈtɑli. Mattiamɑˈtiɑ fearedfɪrd meetingˈmitɪŋ Garofoliˌgærəˈfoʊli, sosoʊ hehi avoidedəˈvɔɪdɪd Rueru Mouffetardmufˈtɑr.

Iaɪ searchedsɜrʧt forfɔr Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃ atæt threeθri boardingˈbɔrdɪŋ housesˈhaʊzɪz: Pajotpɑˈʒoʊ, Barrabaudbɑrɑˈboʊ, andænd Chopinetʃɔpɪˈneɪ. Atæt theðə firstfɜrst twotu, theyðeɪ knewnu nothingˈnʌθɪŋ.

Atæt Chopinet'sʃɔpɪˈneɪz, aə customerˈkʌstəmər toldtoʊld memi Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃ waswʌz stayingˈsteɪɪŋ atæt Hotelhoʊˈtɛl Cantalkɑ̃ˈtɑl, onɑn Rueru Austerlitzˈɔstərˌlɪts.

Beforebɪˈfɔr goingˈgoʊɪŋ thereðɛr, Iaɪ stoppedstɑpt totu askæsk aboutəˈbaʊt Garofoliˌgærəˈfoʊli onɑn Rueru Lourcinelʊrˈsin. Anən oldoʊld manmæn toldtoʊld memi Garofoliˌgærəˈfoʊli stillstɪl hadhæd threeθri monthsmʌnθs leftlɛft inɪn prisonˈprɪzən.

Iaɪ wentwɛnt totu findfaɪnd Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃ atæt Hotelhoʊˈtɛl Cantalkɑ̃ˈtɑl. Theðə landladyˈlændˌleɪdi waswʌz anən oldoʊld womanˈwʊmən withwɪð aə shakingˈʃeɪkɪŋ headhɛd andænd poorpʊr hearingˈhɪrɪŋ.

Theðə oldoʊld womanˈwʊmən criedkraɪd outaʊt: "Ohoʊ deardɪr! Poorpʊr Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃! Hehi dieddaɪd eighteɪt daysdeɪz agoəˈgoʊ, atæt Saint-Antoinesæ̃tɑ̃ˈtwan hospitalˈhɑspɪtəl."

Iaɪ feltfɛlt theðə bloodblʌd draindreɪn fromfrʌm mymaɪ facefeɪs. Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃ waswʌz deaddɛd! Howhaʊ wouldwʊd Iaɪ everˈɛvər findfaɪnd mymaɪ familyˈfæməli nownaʊ?

Theðə oldoʊld womanˈwʊmən saidsɛd Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃ hadhæd toldtoʊld herhɜr hehi hadhæd foundfaʊnd aə childʧaɪld andænd raisedreɪzd itɪt, andænd nownaʊ theðə wealthyˈwɛlθi familyˈfæməli wantedˈwɑntɪd theirðɛr childʧaɪld backbæk.

Butbʌt Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃ hadhæd keptkɛpt theðə nameneɪm andænd addressəˈdrɛs ofʌv mymaɪ familyˈfæməli secretˈsiːkrɪt, wantingˈwɑntɪŋ theðə rewardrɪˈwɔrd allɔl forfɔr himselfhɪmˈsɛlf.

Iaɪ wanderedˈwɑndərd sadlyˈsædli totu theðə Botanicalbəˈtænɪkəl Gardenˈgɑrdən. Thereðɛr Iaɪ sawsɔ aə smallsmɔl happyˈhæpi familyˈfæməli andænd mymaɪ tearstɪrz flowedfloʊd.

Atæt sevenˈsɛvən o'clockəˈklɑk, Iaɪ heardhɜrd Capiˈkɑpi barkbɑrk joyfullyˈʤɔɪfəli. Mattiamɑˈtiɑ appearedəˈpɪrd callingˈkɔlɪŋ: "Wellwɛl?" Iaɪ answeredˈænsərd: "Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃ isɪz deaddɛd!"

Mattiamɑˈtiɑ comfortedˈkʌmfərtɪd memi: "Yourjʊr parentsˈpɛrənts willwɪl worryˈwɜri whenwɛn theyðeɪ hearhɪr nothingˈnʌθɪŋ fromfrʌm Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃. They'llðeɪl comekʌm totu Hotelhoʊˈtɛl Cantalkɑ̃ˈtɑl. Justʤʌst aə fewfju days'deɪz delaydɪˈleɪ!"

Whenwɛn Iaɪ toldtoʊld himhɪm Garofoliˌgærəˈfoʊli hadhæd threeθri monthsmʌnθs leftlɛft inɪn prisonˈprɪzən, Mattiamɑˈtiɑ danceddænst inɪn theðə streetstrit, singingˈsɪŋɪŋ withwɪð joyʤɔɪ.

Wewi lodgedlɑʤd atæt Hotelhoʊˈtɛl Cantalkɑ̃ˈtɑl, inɪn anən atticˈætɪk roomrum sosoʊ tinyˈtaɪni thatðæt whenwɛn onewʌn stoodstʊd, theðə otherˈʌðər hadhæd totu sitsɪt onɑn theðə bedbɛd.
