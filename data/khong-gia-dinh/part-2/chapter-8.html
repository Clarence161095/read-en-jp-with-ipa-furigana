THEðə PRINCE'Sˈprɪnsɪz COWkaʊ

Hehi hadhæd refusedrɪˈfjuzd aə comfortableˈkʌmfərtəbəl lifelaɪf — finefaɪn clotheskloʊðz, goodgʊd foodfud, andænd aə softsɔft bedbɛd — justʤʌst totu staysteɪ withwɪð memi. Addedˈædɪd totu ouraʊər previousˈpriviəs savingsˈseɪvɪŋz, wewi nownaʊ hadhæd aə totalˈtoʊtəl ofʌv twotu hundredˈhʌndrɪd andænd fourteenˌfɔrˈtin francsfræŋks. Thatðæt waswʌz enoughɪˈnʌf totu buybaɪ aə cowkaʊ forfɔr Motherˈmʌðər Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃! Mymaɪ hearthɑrt racedreɪst withwɪð joyʤɔɪ atæt theðə thoughtθɔt thatðæt mymaɪ long-heldlɔŋ hɛld dreamdrim waswʌz aboutəˈbaʊt totu comekʌm truetru.

Butbʌt wewi worriedˈwɜrid aboutəˈbaʊt beingˈbiɪŋ cheatedˈʧitɪd bybaɪ cattleˈkætəl dealersˈdilərz. Wewi hadhæd heardhɜrd ofʌv dishonestdɪsˈɑnɪst tradersˈtreɪdərz. Theyðeɪ couldkʊd attachəˈtæʧ falsefɔls tailsteɪlz totu cowskaʊz, orɔr pumppʌmp upʌp theðə uddersˈʌdərz totu makemeɪk themðɛm looklʊk fullfʊl ofʌv milkmɪlk. Howhaʊ couldkʊd twotu childrenˈʧɪldrən likelaɪk usʌs telltɛl aə goodgʊd cowkaʊ fromfrʌm aə badbæd onewʌn?

Wewi decideddɪˈsaɪdɪd totu gogoʊ totu theðə towntaʊn ofʌv Usselyˈsɛl andænd askæsk aə veterinarianˌvɛtərɪˈnɛriən forfɔr helphɛlp. Iaɪ toldtoʊld himhɪm mymaɪ storyˈstɔri — thatðæt Iaɪ wantedˈwɑntɪd totu buybaɪ aə cowkaʊ asæz aə giftgɪft forfɔr mymaɪ fosterˈfɔstər motherˈmʌðər — andænd hehi agreedəˈgrid totu helphɛlp forfɔr freefri.

Totu showʃoʊ ouraʊər gratitudeˈgrætɪˌtud, thatðæt eveningˈivnɪŋ wewi playedpleɪd aə serenadeˌsɛrəˈneɪd inɪn frontfrʌnt ofʌv hishɪz househaʊs. Hehi openedˈoʊpənd hishɪz windowˈwɪndoʊ totu listenˈlɪsən andænd applaudedəˈplɔdɪd. Wewi werewɜr delighteddɪˈlaɪtɪd thatðæt hehi enjoyedɪnˈʤɔɪd ouraʊər musicˈmjuzɪk.

Onɑn theðə daydeɪ ofʌv theðə cattleˈkætəl fairfɛr, theðə veterinarianˌvɛtərɪˈnɛriən ledlɛd usʌs throughθru theðə marketˈmɑrkɪt. Hehi choseʧoʊz aə smallsmɔl Rouergueruˈɛrg cowkaʊ withwɪð redrɛd hairhɛr andænd gentleˈʤɛntəl eyesaɪz. Hehi saidsɛd: "Thisðɪs cowkaʊ isɪz smallsmɔl butbʌt givesgɪvz plentyˈplɛnti ofʌv milkmɪlk. Thisðɪs isɪz theðə bestbɛst breedbrid forfɔr youju."

Theðə bargainingˈbɑrgənɪŋ beganbɪˈgæn. Theðə dealerˈdilər askedæskt forfɔr threeθri hundredˈhʌndrɪd francsfræŋks. Theðə veterinarianˌvɛtərɪˈnɛriən offeredˈɔfərd twotu hundredˈhʌndrɪd. Theyðeɪ wentwɛnt backbæk andænd forthfɔrθ forfɔr aə longlɔŋ timetaɪm. Finallyˈfaɪnəli, theyðeɪ agreedəˈgrid onɑn twotu hundredˈhʌndrɪd andænd tentɛn francsfræŋks. Mymaɪ handshændz trembledˈtrɛmbəld asæz Iaɪ countedˈkaʊntɪd outaʊt theðə moneyˈmʌni. Itɪt waswʌz nearlyˈnɪrli everythingˈɛvriˌθɪŋ wewi hadhæd savedseɪvd.

Mattiamɑˈtiɑ wantedˈwɑntɪd totu testtɛst ifɪf theðə tailteɪl waswʌz realriəl, sosoʊ hehi grabbedgræbd itɪt andænd pulledpʊld hardhɑrd. Theðə cowkaʊ kickedkɪkt himhɪm rightraɪt inɪn theðə bellyˈbɛli. Hehi fellfɛl totu theðə groundgraʊnd. Everyoneˈɛvriˌwʌn burstbɜrst outaʊt laughingˈlæfɪŋ. Butbʌt Mattiamɑˈtiɑ gotgɑt upʌp, rubbedrʌbd hishɪz bellyˈbɛli, andænd saidsɛd: "Theðə tailteɪl isɪz realriəl!"

Allɔl ofʌv thatðæt ateeɪt upʌp ouraʊər remainingrɪˈmeɪnɪŋ moneyˈmʌni. Wewi werewɜr leftlɛft withwɪð nothingˈnʌθɪŋ — notnɑt aə singleˈsɪŋgəl centimesɑ̃ˈtim. Wewi werewɜr princesˈprɪnsɪz withwɪð aə cowkaʊ butbʌt nonoʊ moneyˈmʌni forfɔr foodfud!

Wewi hadhæd totu performpərˈfɔrm rightraɪt thereðɛr atæt theðə fairfɛr totu earnɜrn moneyˈmʌni forfɔr foodfud. Capiˈkɑpi wentwɛnt aroundəˈraʊnd withwɪð aə hathæt collectingkəˈlɛktɪŋ coinskɔɪnz fromfrʌm theðə audienceˈɔdiəns. Wewi collectedkəˈlɛktɪd sevenˈsɛvən francsfræŋks andænd tentɛn centimessɑ̃ˈtim. Thatðæt waswʌz enoughɪˈnʌf forfɔr foodfud onɑn theðə roadroʊd.

Thatðæt eveningˈivnɪŋ, wewi milkedmɪlkt theðə cowkaʊ. Mattiamɑˈtiɑ tooktʊk aə sipsɪp ofʌv theðə freshfrɛʃ milkmɪlk andænd praisedpreɪzd itɪt highlyˈhaɪli. Hehi saidsɛd theðə milkmɪlk tastedˈteɪstɪd likelaɪk orangeˈɔrɪnʤ blossomsˈblɑsəmz. Iaɪ laughedlæft, forfɔr Iaɪ hadhæd neverˈnɛvər tastedˈteɪstɪd orangeˈɔrɪnʤ blossomsˈblɑsəmz, butbʌt theðə milkmɪlk waswʌz trulyˈtruli deliciousdɪˈlɪʃəs.

Theðə nextnɛkst morningˈmɔrnɪŋ, wewi setsɛt outaʊt forfɔr Chavanonʃɑvɑˈnɔ̃ totu bringbrɪŋ theðə cowkaʊ totu Motherˈmʌðər Barberinbɑrbəˈræ̃. Iaɪ ledlɛd theðə cowkaʊ whilewaɪl Mattiamɑˈtiɑ walkedwɔkt besidebɪˈsaɪd memi. Theðə cowkaʊ followedˈfɑloʊd usʌs obedientlyoʊˈbidiəntli, stoppingˈstɑpɪŋ nownaʊ andænd thenðɛn totu nibbleˈnɪbəl grassgræs bybaɪ theðə roadsideˈroʊdˌsaɪd.
