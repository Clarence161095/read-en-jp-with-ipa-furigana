YOUNGjʌŋ ARTHURˈɑrθər

Iaɪ satsæt inɪn theðə jailʤeɪl cellsɛl, confusedkənˈfjuzd andænd hopelessˈhoʊpləs. Theðə coldkoʊld stonestoʊn wallswɔlz surroundedsəˈraʊndɪd memi. Iaɪ couldkʊd notnɑt understandˌʌndərˈstænd whywaɪ Iaɪ waswʌz herehɪr.

Iaɪ waswʌz accusedəˈkjuzd ofʌv robbingˈrɑbɪŋ theðə Saint-Georgesseɪnt ˈʤɔrʤɪz churchʧɜrʧ. Butbʌt Iaɪ waswʌz innocentˈɪnəsənt! Iaɪ hadhæd onlyˈoʊnli walkedwɔkt pastpæst atæt nightnaɪt andænd Capiˈkɑpi hadhæd runrʌn intoˈɪntu theðə churchʧɜrʧ.

Iaɪ wonderedˈwʌndərd ifɪf Mattiamɑˈtiɑ andænd Bobbɑb couldkʊd helphɛlp memi fromfrʌm outsideˌaʊtˈsaɪd. Perhapspərˈhæps theyðeɪ werewɜr findingˈfaɪndɪŋ aə wayweɪ totu saveseɪv memi. Thatðæt thoughtθɔt gavegeɪv memi aə littleˈlɪtəl hopehoʊp.

Iaɪ rememberedrɪˈmɛmbərd storiesˈstɔriz aboutəˈbaʊt prisonersˈprɪzənərz wherewɛr friendsfrɛndz hidhɪd messagesˈmɛsɪʤɪz inɪn foodfud. Iaɪ checkedʧɛkt everyˈɛvri piecepis ofʌv breadbrɛd, butbʌt Iaɪ foundfaʊnd nothingˈnʌθɪŋ.

Thatðæt nightnaɪt waswʌz terribleˈtɛrəbəl. Iaɪ layleɪ onɑn theðə hardhɑrd woodenˈwʊdən bedbɛd, staringˈstɛrɪŋ atæt theðə darkdɑrk ceilingˈsilɪŋ. Iaɪ thoughtθɔt aboutəˈbaʊt mymaɪ pastpæst mistakesmɪˈsteɪks.

Iaɪ shouldʃʊd havehæv listenedˈlɪsənd totu Mattiamɑˈtiɑ. Hehi hadhæd warnedwɔrnd memi notnɑt totu taketeɪk thatðæt roadroʊd atæt nightnaɪt. Butbʌt Iaɪ diddɪd notnɑt listenˈlɪsən, andænd nownaʊ Iaɪ waswʌz payingˈpeɪɪŋ theðə pricepraɪs.

Theðə nextnɛkst morningˈmɔrnɪŋ, aə jailerˈʤeɪlər broughtbrɔt aə basinˈbeɪsən ofʌv waterˈwɔtər andænd toldtoʊld memi totu washwɑʃ mymaɪ facefeɪs. "Youju gogoʊ totu courtkɔrt todaytəˈdeɪ," hehi saidsɛd. "Makemeɪk yourselfjɔrˈsɛlf cleanklin."

Iaɪ washedwɑʃt mymaɪ facefeɪs andænd combedkoʊmd mymaɪ hairhɛr withwɪð mymaɪ fingersˈfɪŋgərz. Mymaɪ hearthɑrt beatbit veryˈvɛri fastfæst. Iaɪ wouldwʊd havehæv totu standstænd beforebɪˈfɔr theðə judgeʤʌʤ andænd explainɪkˈspleɪn everythingˈɛvriˌθɪŋ.

Theðə jailerˈʤeɪlər ledlɛd memi throughθru longlɔŋ, darkdɑrk corridorsˈkɔrɪdɔrz. Atæt lastlæst, wewi reachedriʧt theðə courtroomˈkɔrtˌrum. Itɪt waswʌz aə largelɑrʤ roomrum withwɪð aə highhaɪ ceilingˈsilɪŋ.

Theðə judgeʤʌʤ satsæt onɑn aə highhaɪ platformˈplætˌfɔrm, togethertəˈgɛðər withwɪð threeθri courtkɔrt officialsəˈfɪʃəlz. Belowbɪˈloʊ, thereðɛr werewɜr rowsroʊz ofʌv benchesˈbɛnʧɪz forfɔr witnessesˈwɪtnɪsɪz andænd spectatorsˈspɛkˌteɪtərz.

Iaɪ lookedlʊkt aroundəˈraʊnd andænd sawsɔ Bobbɑb andænd hishɪz friendsfrɛndz onɑn theðə witnessˈwɪtnɪs benchbɛnʧ. Theyðeɪ hadhæd comekʌm totu helphɛlp memi. Iaɪ feltfɛlt deeplyˈdipli gratefulˈgreɪtfəl.

Thenðɛn Iaɪ lookedlʊkt upʌp atæt theðə balconyˈbælkəni andænd spottedˈspɑtɪd Mattiamɑˈtiɑ. Ouraʊr eyesaɪz metmɛt. Hehi noddedˈnɑdɪd slightlyˈslaɪtli, asæz ifɪf totu sayseɪ: "Bebi bravebreɪv, Rémireɪˈmi!" Thatðæt gavegeɪv memi courageˈkɜrɪʤ.

Iaɪ alsoˈɔlsoʊ hadhæd aə lawyerˈlɔjər. Iaɪ diddɪd notnɑt knownoʊ whohu hadhæd hiredˈhaɪərd himhɪm — perhapspərˈhæps Mattiamɑˈtiɑ andænd Bobbɑb hadhæd foundfaʊnd himhɪm. Hehi waswʌz aə youngjʌŋ manmæn withwɪð brightbraɪt eyesaɪz andænd aə quickkwɪk mindmaɪnd.

Theðə prosecutorˈprɑsɪˌkjutər stoodstʊd upʌp andænd readrɛd theðə chargesˈʧɑrʤɪz. Hehi saidsɛd Iaɪ hadhæd robbedrɑbd theðə Saint-Georgesseɪnt ˈʤɔrʤɪz churchʧɜrʧ andænd usedjuzd aə dogdɔg asæz aə lookoutˈlʊkˌaʊt whilewaɪl stealingˈstilɪŋ.

Theðə judgeʤʌʤ turnedtɜrnd totu memi andænd askedæskt ifɪf Iaɪ understoodˌʌndərˈstʊd Englishˈɪŋglɪʃ. Iaɪ understoodˌʌndərˈstʊd aə littleˈlɪtəl, butbʌt Iaɪ askedæskt totu speakspik inɪn Frenchfrɛnʧ. Luckilyˈlʌkɪli, theðə judgeʤʌʤ knewnju Frenchfrɛnʧ.

Iaɪ toldtoʊld mymaɪ storyˈstɔri. Iaɪ hadhæd beenbɪn atæt theðə fairgroundˈfɛrˌgraʊnd performingpərˈfɔrmɪŋ untilənˈtɪl onewʌn inɪn theðə morningˈmɔrnɪŋ. Afterˈæftər thatðæt, Iaɪ walkedwɔkt totu theðə Oakoʊk Treetri innɪn totu sleepslip forfɔr theðə nightnaɪt.

Theðə judgeʤʌʤ askedæskt memi: "Thenðɛn howhaʊ diddɪd yourjʊr dogdɔg endɛnd upʌp insideɪnˈsaɪd theðə churchʧɜrʧ?" Thatðæt waswʌz aə difficultˈdɪfɪˌkʌlt questionˈkwɛsʧən. Iaɪ explainedɪkˈspleɪnd thatðæt Capiˈkɑpi oftenˈɔfən ranræn offɔf totu exploreɪkˈsplɔr everywhereˈɛvriˌwɛr.

Thenðɛn theðə churchʧɜrʧ wardenˈwɔrdən waswʌz calledkɔld totu testifyˈtɛstɪˌfaɪ. Hehi waswʌz aə red-facedrɛd feɪst, fatfæt manmæn whohu lookedlʊkt veryˈvɛri pompousˈpɑmpəs andænd arrogantˈærəgənt.

Hehi saidsɛd hehi hadhæd foundfaʊnd theðə dogdɔg insideɪnˈsaɪd theðə churchʧɜrʧ earlyˈɜrli thatðæt morningˈmɔrnɪŋ, alongəˈlɔŋ withwɪð signssaɪnz ofʌv theðə robberyˈrɑbəri. Hehi insistedɪnˈsɪstɪd hehi hadhæd lockedlɑkt theðə doorsdɔrz carefullyˈkɛrfəli theðə nightnaɪt beforebɪˈfɔr.

Butbʌt mymaɪ lawyerˈlɔjər waswʌz brilliantˈbrɪljənt. Hehi beganbɪˈgæn totu questionˈkwɛsʧən theðə churchʧɜrʧ wardenˈwɔrdən withwɪð sharpʃɑrp questionsˈkwɛsʧənz, makingˈmeɪkɪŋ theðə manmæn nervousˈnɜrvəs.

"Dodu youju havehæv goodgʊd eyesightˈaɪˌsaɪt?" theðə lawyerˈlɔjər askedæskt. "Oncewʌns youju walkedwɔkt intoˈɪntu aə calfkæf carcassˈkɑrkəs atæt theðə butcher'sˈbʊʧərz becausebɪˈkɔz youju couldkʊd notnɑt seesi itɪt, didn'tˈdɪdənt youju?" Theðə courtroomˈkɔrtˌrum laughedlæft.

Theðə warden'sˈwɔrdənz facefeɪs turnedtɜrnd evenˈivən redderˈrɛdər. Theðə lawyerˈlɔjər continuedkənˈtɪnjud: "Youju drinkdrɪŋk strongstrɔŋ beerbɪr, don'tdoʊnt youju? Sometimesˈsʌmˌtaɪmz fourfɔr totu sixsɪks largelɑrʤ bottlesˈbɑtəlz atæt aə timetaɪm?"

"Andænd afterˈæftər dinnerˈdɪnər, youju alsoˈɔlsoʊ drinkdrɪŋk spiritsˈspɪrɪts?" theðə lawyerˈlɔjər pressedprɛst. Theðə wardenˈwɔrdən stammeredˈstæmərd andænd diddɪd notnɑt knownoʊ whatwʌt totu answerˈænsər.

Theðə lawyerˈlɔjər concludedkənˈkludɪd: "Sosoʊ withwɪð poorpʊr eyesightˈaɪˌsaɪt andænd aə drinkingˈdrɪŋkɪŋ habitˈhæbɪt, itɪt isɪz veryˈvɛri likelyˈlaɪkli thatðæt youju forgotfərˈgɑt totu locklɑk theðə churchʧɜrʧ doordɔr. Theðə dogdɔg simplyˈsɪmpli wanderedˈwɑndərd inɪn onɑn itsɪts ownoʊn."

Otherˈʌðər witnessesˈwɪtnɪsɪz spokespoʊk onewʌn bybaɪ onewʌn. Theyðeɪ confirmedkənˈfɜrmd thatðæt Iaɪ hadhæd performedpərˈfɔrmd atæt theðə fairgroundˈfɛrˌgraʊnd untilənˈtɪl lateleɪt. Theðə innkeeperˈɪnˌkipər saidsɛd Iaɪ arrivedəˈraɪvd nearnɪr twotu inɪn theðə morningˈmɔrnɪŋ.

Iaɪ beganbɪˈgæn totu hopehoʊp. Theðə evidenceˈɛvɪdəns clearlyˈklɪrli showedʃoʊd Iaɪ waswʌz innocentˈɪnəsənt. Theðə lawyerˈlɔjər hadhæd donedʌn aə wonderfulˈwʌndərfəl jobʤɑb. Surelyˈʃʊrli theðə judgeʤʌʤ wouldwʊd setsɛt memi freefri.

Iaɪ waswʌz devastatedˈdɛvəˌsteɪtɪd. Evenˈivən thoughðoʊ theðə evidenceˈɛvɪdəns showedʃoʊd mymaɪ innocenceˈɪnəsəns, Iaɪ stillstɪl hadhæd totu staysteɪ inɪn jailʤeɪl andænd waitweɪt. Iaɪ wouldwʊd facefeɪs theðə assizesəˈsaɪzɪz — wherewɛr seriousˈsɪriəs criminalsˈkrɪmɪnəlz werewɜr judgedʤʌʤd.
