FORWARDˈfɔrwərd

Forwardˈfɔrwərd!

Theðə worldwɜrld layleɪ openˈoʊpən beforebɪˈfɔr memi. Iaɪ couldkʊd gogoʊ southsaʊθ orɔr northnɔrθ, eastist orɔr westwɛst, asæz Iaɪ pleasedplizd. Iaɪ waswʌz onlyˈoʊnli aə childʧaɪld, yetjɛt Iaɪ waswʌz masterˈmæstər ofʌv mymaɪ ownoʊn lifelaɪf. Butbʌt thatðæt waswʌz theðə sadsæd partpɑrt ofʌv mymaɪ situationˌsɪʧuˈeɪʃən.

Howhaʊ manyˈmɛni childrenˈʧɪldrən sayseɪ: "Ifɪf onlyˈoʊnli Iaɪ werewɜr freefri! Ifɪf onlyˈoʊnli Iaɪ couldkʊd bebi mymaɪ ownoʊn masterˈmæstər!" Butbʌt Iaɪ saidsɛd totu myselfmaɪˈsɛlf: "Ifɪf onlyˈoʊnli someoneˈsʌmwʌn wouldwʊd guidegaɪd memi andænd showʃoʊ memi theðə wayweɪ!"

Thereðɛr waswʌz aə terribleˈtɛrəbəl differenceˈdɪfərəns betweenbɪˈtwin thoseðoʊz childrenˈʧɪldrən andænd memi. Ifɪf theyðeɪ mademeɪd aə foolishˈfulɪʃ mistakemɪsˈteɪk, someoneˈsʌmwʌn waswʌz thereðɛr totu catchkæʧ themðɛm whenwɛn theyðeɪ fellfɛl. Behindbɪˈhaɪnd memi, thereðɛr waswʌz nonoʊ onewʌn. Ifɪf Iaɪ fellfɛl, Iaɪ hadhæd totu getgɛt upʌp aloneəˈloʊn.

Beforebɪˈfɔr settingˈsɛtɪŋ outaʊt, Iaɪ wantedˈwɑntɪd totu askæsk Fatherˈfɑðər Acquinəˈkwɪn forfɔr adviceædˈvaɪs, theðə manmæn whohu hadhæd treatedˈtritɪd memi likelaɪk hishɪz ownoʊn sonsʌn. Iaɪ diddɪd notnɑt daredɛr walkwɔk throughθru Parisˈpærɪs withwɪð Capiˈkɑpi looselus atæt mymaɪ heelshilz, fearingˈfɪrɪŋ theðə policepəˈlis. Iaɪ foundfaʊnd aə leashliʃ forfɔr theðə dogdɑg andænd wentwɛnt totu findfaɪnd theðə Clichykliˈʃi prisonˈprɪzən.

Theyðeɪ ledlɛd memi intoˈɪntu aə visitingˈvɪzɪtɪŋ roomrum withoutwɪˈθaʊt ironˈaɪərn barsbɑrz asæz Iaɪ hadhæd imaginedɪˈmæʤɪnd. Soonsun Fatherˈfɑðər Acquinəˈkwɪn appearedəˈpɪrd, withoutwɪˈθaʊt chainsʧeɪnz onɑn hishɪz handshændz orɔr feetfit.

"Iaɪ waswʌz hopingˈhoʊpɪŋ youju wouldwʊd comekʌm, Remiˈrɛmi," hehi saidsɛd.

Hehi advisedædˈvaɪzd memi totu findfaɪnd workwɜrk asæz aə laborerˈleɪbərər ratherˈræðər thanðæn wanderˈwɑndər asæz aə streetstrit musicianmjuˈzɪʃən — itɪt waswʌz tootu dangerousˈdeɪnʤərəs. Butbʌt Iaɪ hadhæd promisedˈprɑmɪst totu visitˈvɪzɪt hishɪz scatteredˈskætərd childrenˈʧɪldrən, especiallyɪˈspɛʃəli Liseliz. Ifɪf Iaɪ brokebroʊk mymaɪ promiseˈprɑmɪs outaʊt ofʌv fearfɪr, Iaɪ wouldwʊd bebi thinkingˈθɪŋkɪŋ onlyˈoʊnli ofʌv myselfmaɪˈsɛlf, notnɑt ofʌv themðɛm.

Hehi tooktʊk mymaɪ handshændz: "Iaɪ mustmʌst kisskɪs youju forfɔr thatðæt. Youju areɑr aə loyalˈlɔɪəl andænd faithfulˈfeɪθfəl boybɔɪ."

Hehi tooktʊk fromfrʌm hishɪz vestvɛst pocketˈpɑkɪt aə largelɑrʤ pocketˈpɑkɪt watchwɑʧ. "Thisðɪs watchwɑʧ isɪz allɔl Iaɪ havehæv leftlɛft. Iaɪ givegɪv itɪt totu youju." Hehi placedpleɪst theðə watchwɑʧ inɪn mymaɪ handhænd.

"Inɪn prisonˈprɪzən, Iaɪ havehæv nonoʊ neednid totu knownoʊ theðə timetaɪm. Watchingˈwɑʧɪŋ theðə hoursaʊərz onlyˈoʊnli makesmeɪks themðɛm passpæs slowerˈsloʊər."

"Goodbyeˌgʊdˈbaɪ, Remiˈrɛmi. Youju areɑr aə goodgʊd boybɔɪ. Staysteɪ thatðæt wayweɪ alwaysˈɔlˌweɪz."

Iaɪ hadhæd aə watchwɑʧ! Aə watchwɑʧ ofʌv mymaɪ veryˈvɛri ownoʊn! Sadnessˈsædnɪs, worryˈwɜri, andænd fearfɪr vanishedˈvænɪʃt instantlyˈɪnstəntli. Iaɪ pulledpʊld theðə watchwɑʧ outaʊt andænd checkedʧɛkt: exactlyɪgˈzæktli noonnun. Capiˈkɑpi stoodstʊd upʌp, staredstɛrd atæt theðə watchwɑʧ, thenðɛn barkedbɑrkt twelvetwɛlv timestaɪmz. Hehi rememberedrɪˈmɛmbərd!

Iaɪ enteredˈɛntərd Rueru Mouffetardmufˈtɑr. Memoriesˈmɛməriz floodedˈflʌdɪd backbæk: Garofoliˌgɑrəˈfoʊli, Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ, oldoʊld Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs.

Nearnɪr theðə churchʧɜrʧ ofʌv Saint-Medardsæ̃ meɪˈdɑr, Iaɪ sawsɔ aə boybɔɪ leaningˈlinɪŋ againstəˈgɛnst theðə wallwɔl whohu lookedlʊkt likelaɪk Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ. Iaɪ camekeɪm closerˈkloʊsər. Itɪt waswʌz indeedɪnˈdid himhɪm.

"Garofoliˌgɑrəˈfoʊli isɪz inɪn prisonˈprɪzən," hehi saidsɛd. "Theyðeɪ caughtkɔt himhɪm becausebɪˈkɔz hehi beatbit Orlandoɔrˈlændoʊ sosoʊ badlyˈbædli theðə boybɔɪ dieddaɪd."

Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ explainedɪksˈpleɪnd thatðæt Garofoliˌgɑrəˈfoʊli hadhæd rentedˈrɛntɪd himhɪm totu theðə Gassotgæˈsoʊ circusˈsɜrkəs, butbʌt theyðeɪ dismisseddɪsˈmɪst himhɪm becausebɪˈkɔz hishɪz headhɛd waswʌz tootu bigbɪg totu fitfɪt inɪn theðə boxbɑks anymoreˌɛniˈmɔr. "Iaɪ havehæv notnɑt eatenˈitən sincesɪns yesterdayˈjɛstərˌdeɪ noonnun!"

Iaɪ ranræn totu theðə bakeryˈbeɪkəri andænd boughtbɔt himhɪm aə loafloʊf. Hehi devoureddɪˈvaʊərd itɪt.

"Pleasepliz taketeɪk memi intoˈɪntu yourjʊr troupetrup," Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ beggedbɛgd. "Iaɪ cankæn performpərˈfɔrm. Iaɪ playpleɪ violinˌvaɪəˈlɪn, dodu acrobaticsˌækrəˈbætɪks, jumpʤʌmp roperoʊp. Iaɪ onlyˈoʊnli neednid foodfud. Ifɪf Iaɪ dodu badlyˈbædli, hithɪt memi. Justʤʌst notnɑt onɑn theðə headhɛd."

Hearingˈhɪrɪŋ himhɪm sayseɪ thatðæt, Iaɪ nearlyˈnɪrli criedkraɪd. Iaɪ saidsɛd: "Agreedəˈgrid. Comekʌm withwɪð memi, notnɑt asæz aə servantˈsɜrvənt butbʌt asæz aə friendfrɛnd."

Fifteenfɪfˈtin minutesˈmɪnɪts laterˈleɪtər, wewi leftlɛft Parisˈpærɪs. Theðə Marchmɑrʧ sunsʌn hadhæd drieddraɪd theðə roadsroʊdz. Theðə Aprilˈeɪprəl skyskaɪ waswʌz brightbraɪt andænd cloudlessˈklaʊdlɪs. Grassgræs grewgru bybaɪ theðə roadsideˈroʊdˌsaɪd, dottedˈdɑtɪd withwɪð daisiesˈdeɪziz andænd wildwaɪld strawberryˈstrɔˌbɛri flowersˈflaʊərz.

Wewi restedˈrɛstɪd onɑn aə pilepaɪl ofʌv gravelˈgrævəl. Iaɪ spreadsprɛd mymaɪ mapmæp onɑn theðə grassgræs andænd plannedplænd ouraʊər routerut. Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ pointedˈpɔɪntɪd atæt theðə mapmæp: "Whatwʌt isɪz thisðɪs?" Hehi couldkʊd notnɑt readrid. Iaɪ promisedˈprɑmɪst totu teachtiʧ himhɪm.

Iaɪ gavegeɪv himhɪm twotu shirtsʃɜrts, twotu pairspɛrz ofʌv sockssɑks, andænd threeθri handkerchiefsˈhæŋkərˌʧɪfs. Thenðɛn Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ playedpleɪd hishɪz violinˌvaɪəˈlɪn forfɔr memi. Hehi playedpleɪd nearlyˈnɪrli asæz wellwɛl asæz oldoʊld Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs. Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ waswʌz aə treasureˈtrɛʒər!

Passingˈpæsɪŋ aə farmfɑrm, wewi sawsɔ aə weddingˈwɛdɪŋ celebrationˌsɛləˈbreɪʃən. Aə fatfæt youngjʌŋ manmæn calledkɔld usʌs inɪn totu playpleɪ musicˈmjuzɪk forfɔr theðə dancersˈdænsərz. Wewi playedpleɪd quadrilleskwɑˈdrɪlz andænd waltzesˈwɔltsɪz withoutwɪˈθaʊt stoppingˈstɑpɪŋ untilənˈtɪl eveningˈivnɪŋ.

Theðə bridebraɪd noticedˈnoʊtɪst Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ turningˈtɜrnɪŋ palepeɪl: "Stopstɑp! Theðə boybɔɪ isɪz exhaustedɪgˈzɔstɪd. Paypeɪ theðə musiciansmjuˈzɪʃənz!" Capiˈkɑpi collectedkəˈlɛktɪd moneyˈmʌni inɪn aə hathæt. Theðə groomgrum gavegeɪv fivefaɪv francsfræŋks!

Bybaɪ morningˈmɔrnɪŋ, wewi hadhæd twenty-eightˈtwɛntiˌeɪt francsfræŋks. "Wewi oweoʊ thisðɪs totu youju, Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ," Iaɪ saidsɛd. Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ sometimesˈsʌmˌtaɪmz saidsɛd: "Aə masterˈmæstər whohu neverˈnɛvər beatsbits hishɪz peopleˈpipəl — howhaʊ wonderfulˈwʌndərfəl!"

Iaɪ boughtbɔt Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ aə trombonetrɑmˈboʊn, redrɛd ribbonsˈrɪbənz forfɔr ouraʊər stockingsˈstɑkɪŋz, andænd anən oldoʊld armyˈɑrmi bagbæg forfɔr himhɪm.

Thenðɛn aə wonderfulˈwʌndərfəl ideaaɪˈdiə camekeɪm totu memi: buybaɪ Motherˈmʌðər Barberinbɑrbərɪn aə milkmɪlk cowkaʊ totu replacerɪˈpleɪs Roussetteruˈsɛt! Howhaʊ happyˈhæpi sheʃi wouldwʊd bebi!

Iaɪ askedæskt aə cattleˈkætəl herderˈhɜrdər theðə pricepraɪs ofʌv aə milkmɪlk cowkaʊ. Hehi laughedlæft inɪn mymaɪ facefeɪs. Finallyˈfaɪnəli hehi saidsɛd seriouslyˈsɪriəsli: onewʌn hundredˈhʌndrɪd andænd fiftyˈfɪfti francsfræŋks. Iaɪ waswʌz farfɑr fromfrʌm thatðæt amountəˈmaʊnt! Butbʌt ifɪf ouraʊər lucklʌk heldhɛld, savingˈseɪvɪŋ pennyˈpɛni bybaɪ pennyˈpɛni, wewi wouldwʊd getgɛt thereðɛr.

Ifɪf wewi wentwɛnt totu Varsesvɑrs firstfɜrst, thenðɛn visitedˈvɪzɪtɪd Motherˈmʌðər Barberinbɑrbərɪn onɑn theðə wayweɪ backbæk, wewi wouldwʊd havehæv timetaɪm totu earnɜrn enoughɪˈnʌf.

Nextnɛkst morningˈmɔrnɪŋ, Iaɪ sharedʃɛrd mymaɪ planplæn withwɪð Mattiaˈmɑtiɑ. Hehi agreedəˈgrid atæt oncewʌns. "Theðə miningˈmaɪnɪŋ regionˈriʤən soundssaʊndz excitingɪkˈsaɪtɪŋ," hehi saidsɛd. "I'daɪd lovelʌv totu seesi aə minemaɪn!"
