Atæt twotu inɪn theðə morningˈmɔrnɪŋ, theðə growerˈgroʊər openedˈoʊpənd hishɪz gategeɪt forfɔr marketˈmɑrkɪt andænd foundfaʊnd usʌs underˈʌndər theðə strawstrɔ.

Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs waswʌz deaddɛd! Whenwɛn theðə littleˈlɪtəl girlgɜrl heardhɜrd thisðɪs, sheʃi steppedstɛpt closekloʊs totu herhɜr fatherˈfɑðər. Sheʃi putpʊt onewʌn handhænd onɑn herhɜr fatherˈfɑðər andænd pointedˈpɔɪntɪd atæt memi withwɪð theðə otherˈʌðər, makingˈmeɪkɪŋ aə softsɔft sighsaɪ ofʌv pityˈpɪti.

Herhɜr gestureˈʤɛsʧər waswʌz clearklɪr withoutwɪˈðaʊt wordswɜrdz. Iaɪ feltfɛlt naturalˈnæʧərəl sympathyˈsɪmpəθi inɪn herhɜr eyesaɪz. Sincesɪns partingˈpɑrtɪŋ withwɪð Arthurˈɑrθər, thisðɪs waswʌz theðə firstfɜrst timetaɪm mymaɪ hearthɑrt filledfɪld withwɪð warmthwɔrmθ andænd trusttrʌst.

Theðə fatherˈfɑðər saidsɛd: "Yesjɛs, littleˈlɪtəl Liseliz. Theðə truthtruθ isɪz sadsæd, butbʌt wewi mustmʌst telltɛl himhɪm."

Aə boybɔɪ namedneɪmd Benjaminˈbɛnʤəmɪn saidsɛd Capiˈkɑpi hadhæd followedˈfɑloʊd theðə stretcherˈstrɛʧər, headhɛd downdaʊn, jumpingˈʤʌmpɪŋ ontoˈɑntu itɪt againəˈgɛn andænd againəˈgɛn. Eachiʧ timetaɪm theyðeɪ pushedpʊʃt himhɪm offɔf, hehi criedkraɪd pitifulˈpɪtɪfəl howlshaʊlz thatðæt chokedʧoʊkt inɪn hishɪz throatθroʊt.

Iaɪ stoodstʊd upʌp, tooktʊk mymaɪ harphɑrp, slungslʌŋ itɪt overˈoʊvər mymaɪ shoulderˈʃoʊldər. Iaɪ hadhæd totu gogoʊ — butbʌt wherewɛr? Butbʌt whenwɛn Iaɪ stoodstʊd, Iaɪ staggeredˈstægərd andænd nearlyˈnɪrli fellfɛl, grabbingˈgræbɪŋ aə chairʧɛr.

Theðə familyˈfæməli satsæt aroundəˈraʊnd aə tableˈteɪbəl eatingˈitɪŋ cabbageˈkæbɪʤ soupsup. Theðə smellsmɛl hithɪt memi likelaɪk aə blowbloʊ. Iaɪ hadhæd eatenˈitən nothingˈnʌθɪŋ sincesɪns yesterdayˈjɛstərˌdeɪ. Mymaɪ legslɛgz gavegeɪv wayweɪ.

Iaɪ couldkʊd notnɑt bringbrɪŋ myselfmaɪˈsɛlf totu begbɛg forfɔr soupsup. Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs hadhæd neverˈnɛvər taughttɔt memi totu begbɛg. Iaɪ wouldwʊd ratherˈræðər diedaɪ ofʌv hungerˈhʌŋgər thanðæn sayseɪ: "Iaɪ amæm hungryˈhʌŋgri."

Liseliz satsæt acrossəˈkrɔs fromfrʌm memi, watchingˈwɑʧɪŋ. Suddenlyˈsʌdənli sheʃi stoodstʊd upʌp, carriedˈkærid herhɜr fullfʊl bowlboʊl ofʌv soupsup, andænd placedpleɪst itɪt onɑn mymaɪ laplæp.

Theðə fatherˈfɑðər saidsɛd: "Taketeɪk itɪt! Whenwɛn Liseliz givesgɪvz somethingˈsʌmθɪŋ, sheʃi meansminz itɪt withwɪð allɔl herhɜr hearthɑrt." Iaɪ finishedˈfɪnɪʃt theðə bowlboʊl inɪn anən instantˈɪnstənt. Liseliz tooktʊk itɪt totu herhɜr fatherˈfɑðər forfɔr moremɔr, thenðɛn broughtbrɔt itɪt backbæk withwɪð aə smilesmaɪl.

Herhɜr smilesmaɪl waswʌz sosoʊ gentleˈʤɛntəl thatðæt evenˈivən starvingˈstɑrvɪŋ, Iaɪ frozefroʊz withwɪð emotionɪˈmoʊʃən andænd forgotfərˈgɑt totu taketeɪk theðə bowlboʊl.

"Iaɪ hadhæd nonoʊ dinnerˈdɪnər, nonoʊ lunchlʌnʧ." "Thenðɛn theðə oldoʊld manmæn dieddaɪd ofʌv coldkoʊld ANDænd hungerˈhʌŋgər."

Liseliz tooktʊk mymaɪ handhænd andænd ledlɛd memi totu aə paintingˈpeɪntɪŋ ofʌv Saintseɪnt Johnʤɑn inɪn aə sheepskinˈʃipˌskɪn. Sheʃi strokedstroʊkt theðə sheepskinˈʃipˌskɪn, thenðɛn pointedˈpɔɪntɪd atæt mymaɪ hairhɛr, partedˈpɑrtɪd downdaʊn theðə middleˈmɪdəl likelaɪk Saintseɪnt John'sʤɑnz. "Yesjɛs!" saidsɛd theðə fatherˈfɑðər. "Hehi lookslʊks likelaɪk Saintseɪnt Johnʤɑn!" Liseliz clappedklæpt andænd laughedlæft.

Theðə gardenerˈgɑrdənər offeredˈɔfərd: "Dodu youju wantwɑnt totu staysteɪ? Aə warmwɔrm bedbɛd, aə fireˈfaɪər, andænd workwɜrk." "You'lljul shareʃɛr theðə laborˈleɪbər — riseraɪz earlyˈɜrli, digdɪg allɔl daydeɪ, earnɜrn yourjʊr breadbrɛd withwɪð sweatswɛt." "Butbʌt eachiʧ nightnaɪt aə bedbɛd waitsweɪts, andænd theðə soupsup youju earnedɜrnd yourselfjɔrˈsɛlf tastesteɪsts betterˈbɛtər."

"Ifɪf youju areɑr aə goodgʊd boybɔɪ, thisðɪs familyˈfæməli willwɪl bebi yourjʊr familyˈfæməli." Liseliz turnedtɜrnd andænd smiledsmaɪld atæt memi throughθru herhɜr tearstɪrz.

Iaɪ quicklyˈkwɪkli tooktʊk offɔf mymaɪ harphɑrp. Theðə fatherˈfɑðər laughedlæft: "That'sðæts aə goodgʊd answerˈænsər." "Hanghæŋ itɪt onɑn thatðæt nailneɪl. Whenwɛn youju wantwɑnt totu leaveliv, taketeɪk itɪt downdaʊn."

Theðə househaʊs waswʌz inɪn theðə Glacièreglɑˈsjɛr districtˈdɪstrɪkt. Theðə ownerˈoʊnər waswʌz Pierrepjɛr Acquinɑˈkæ̃ — withwɪð sonssʌnz Alexisəˈlɛksɪs andænd Benjaminˈbɛnʤəmɪn, eldestˈɛldɪst daughterˈdɔtər Étiennetteeɪtjɛˈnɛt, andænd littleˈlɪtəl Liseliz.

Liseliz waswʌz mutemjut — notnɑt fromfrʌm birthbɜrθ. Sheʃi hadhæd spokenˈspoʊkən forfɔr twotu yearsjɪrz, thenðɛn lostlɔst herhɜr voicevɔɪs atæt almostˈɔlˌmoʊst threeθri fromfrʌm terribleˈtɛrəbəl convulsionskənˈvʌlʃənz.
