Theðə performerspərˈfɔrməz inɪn Vitalis'svɪˈtɑlɪsɪz troupetrup — theðə dogsdɑgz andænd theðə monkeyˈmʌŋki — werewɜr trulyˈtruli talentedˈtæləntɪd. Butbʌt theirðɛr trickstrɪks werewɜr limitedˈlɪmɪtɪd. Afterˈæftər threeθri orɔr fourfɔr showsʃoʊz, everyoneˈɛvriˌwʌn knewnu themðɛm allɔl. Sosoʊ wewi couldn'tˈkʊdənt staysteɪ longlɔŋ inɪn onewʌn towntaʊn. Afterˈæftər threeθri daysdeɪz inɪn Usseljuˈsɛl, wewi movedmuvd onɑn.

Iaɪ dareddɛrd totu askæsk: "Wherewɛr areɑr wewi goingˈgoʊɪŋ, sirsɜr?"

"Dodu youju knownoʊ thisðɪs regionˈriʤən?" — "Nonoʊ." — "Thenðɛn whywaɪ askæsk?" — "Totu knownoʊ."

Hehi studiedˈstʌdid memi aə longlɔŋ timetaɪm, thenðɛn askedæskt: "Youju can'tkænt readrid, cankæn youju?"

Hehi explainedɪkˈspleɪnd: booksbʊks holdhoʊld moremɔr thanðæn prayersprɛrz. Peopleˈpipəl writeraɪt downdaʊn whatwʌt theyðeɪ seesi andænd learnlɜrn. "Iaɪ justʤʌst openˈoʊpən aə bookbʊk andænd knownoʊ everythingˈɛvriˌθɪŋ, asæz ifɪf I'daɪd seensin itɪt withwɪð mymaɪ ownoʊn eyesaɪz!"

Hishɪz wordswɜrdz awakenedəˈweɪkənd memi, slowlyˈsloʊli atæt firstfɜrst, thenðɛn withwɪð growingˈgroʊɪŋ clarityˈklærɪti. Iaɪ hadhæd growngroʊn upʌp likelaɪk aə littleˈlɪtəl savageˈsævɪʤ, withwɪð nonoʊ ideaaɪˈdiə ofʌv civilizedˈsɪvəˌlaɪzd lifelaɪf. Motherˈmʌðər Barberinˈbɑrbərɪn sentsɛnt memi totu schoolskul forfɔr justʤʌst onewʌn monthmʌnθ. Iaɪ neverˈnɛvər touchedtʌʧt aə bookbʊk.

Theðə teacherˈtiʧər waswʌz aə clogklɑg-makerˈmeɪkər. Fromfrʌm morningˈmɔrnɪŋ totu nightnaɪt, woodwʊd shavingsˈʃeɪvɪŋz flewflu aroundəˈraʊnd himhɪm. Hishɪz daughterˈdɔtər waswʌz supposedsəˈpoʊzd totu teachtiʧ usʌs, butbʌt sheʃi waswʌz aə seamstressˈsimstrɪs andænd spentspɛnt allɔl daydeɪ sewingˈsoʊɪŋ. Twelvetwɛlv studentsˈstudənts paidpeɪd fiftyˈfɪfti centimessɑnˈtimz eachiʧ — sixsɪks francsfræŋks totalˈtoʊtəl! Notnɑt enoughɪˈnʌf totu feedfid twotu peopleˈpipəl. Sosoʊ Iaɪ learnedlɜrnd nothingˈnʌθɪŋ.

Walkingˈwɔkɪŋ andænd thinkingˈθɪŋkɪŋ, Iaɪ askedæskt: "Isɪz itɪt hardhɑrd totu learnlɜrn totu readrid?"

"Hardhɑrd forfɔr theðə dimdɪm-wittedˈwɪtɪd, harderˈhɑrdər forfɔr theðə unwillingʌnˈwɪlɪŋ. Areɑr youju dimdɪm?"

"Iaɪ don'tdoʊnt knownoʊ, butbʌt determinationdɪˌtɜrməˈneɪʃən — Iaɪ havehæv plentyˈplɛnti ofʌv thatðæt."

Theðə nextnɛkst daydeɪ hehi pickedpɪkt upʌp aə piecepis ofʌv woodwʊd fromfrʌm theðə roadroʊd. "Herehɪr isɪz yourjʊr readingˈridɪŋ bookbʊk."

Aə plankplæŋk — aə bookbʊk? Iaɪ lookedlʊkt atæt himhɪm totu seesi ifɪf hehi waswʌz mockingˈmɑkɪŋ memi. Itɪt waswʌz aə smoothsmuð chestnutˈʧɛsˌnʌt boardbɔrd, asæz longlɔŋ asæz mymaɪ armɑrm. Nothingˈnʌθɪŋ waswʌz writtenˈrɪtən onɑn itɪt.

Hehi smiledsmaɪld: "Yourjʊr mindmaɪnd isɪz workingˈwɜrkɪŋ." — "Areɑr youju makingˈmeɪkɪŋ funfʌn ofʌv memi?"

"Neverˈnɛvər! Mockingˈmɑkɪŋ ignoranceˈɪgnərəns isɪz itselfɪtˈsɛlf foolishˈfulɪʃ. Waitweɪt untilənˈtɪl wewi reachriʧ thoseðoʊz treestriz, andænd you'lljul seesi."

Wewi satsæt onɑn greengrin grassgræs dottedˈdɑtɪd withwɪð snowdropsˈsnoʊˌdrɑps. Joli-Coeurˌʒɑliˈkɜr leapedlipt intoˈɪntu theðə treestriz. Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs tooktʊk hishɪz knifenaɪf, splitsplɪt theðə plankplæŋk intoˈɪntu thinθɪn sheetsʃits, andænd cutkʌt twelvetwɛlv smallsmɔl squaresskwɛrz.

"Onɑn eachiʧ squareskwɛr, I'llaɪl carvekɑrv aə letterˈlɛtər. Learnlɜrn theirðɛr shapesʃeɪps, thenðɛn combinekəmˈbaɪn themðɛm intoˈɪntu wordswɜrdz."

Graduallyˈgræʤuəli Iaɪ memorizedˈmɛməˌraɪzd theðə alphabetˈælfəˌbɛt. Butbʌt readingˈridɪŋ waswʌz anotherəˈnʌðər matterˈmætər. Sometimesˈsʌmˌtaɪmz Iaɪ regrettedrɪˈgrɛtɪd wantingˈwɑntɪŋ totu learnlɜrn.

Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs decideddɪˈsaɪdɪd Capiˈkɑpi couldkʊd learnlɜrn tootu. Sosoʊ wewi studiedˈstʌdid togethertəˈgɛðər. Iaɪ becamebɪˈkeɪm theðə dog'sdɑgz classmateˈklæsˌmeɪt! Capiˈkɑpi couldn'tˈkʊdənt readrid aloudəˈlaʊd — hehi couldn'tˈkʊdənt speakspik. Butbʌt whenwɛn theðə tilestaɪlz werewɜr spreadsprɛd onɑn theðə grassgræs, Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs calledkɔld aə letterˈlɛtər andænd Capiˈkɑpi pawedpɔd itɪt outaʊt.

Atæt firstfɜrst Iaɪ waswʌz quickerˈkwɪkər. Butbʌt hishɪz memoryˈmɛməri heldhɛld firmerˈfɜrmər. Whatwʌt hehi learnedlɜrnd, hehi neverˈnɛvər forgotfərˈgɑt. Whenwɛn Iaɪ stumbledˈstʌmbəld, Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs saidsɛd: "Capiˈkɑpi willwɪl learnlɜrn totu readrid beforebɪˈfɔr Remiˈrɛmi!"

Theðə dogdɑg waggedwægd hishɪz tailteɪl triumphantlytraɪˈʌmfəntli, asæz ifɪf hehi understoodˌʌndərˈstʊd. "Stupiderˈstupɪdər thanðæn anən animalˈænəməl — finefaɪn onɑn stagesteɪʤ, butbʌt shamefulˈʃeɪmfəl inɪn realriəl lifelaɪf."

Thatðæt spurredspɜrd memi onɑn. Poorpʊr Capiˈkɑpi — whilewaɪl hehi stillstɪl pawedpɔd atæt lettersˈlɛtərz, Iaɪ waswʌz alreadyɔlˈrɛdi readingˈridɪŋ booksbʊks.

"Nownaʊ thatðæt youju cankæn readrid, shallʃæl wewi learnlɜrn musicˈmjuzɪk?"

"Cankæn Iaɪ learnlɜrn totu singsɪŋ? Whenwɛn youju singsɪŋ, Iaɪ amæm completelykəmˈplitli inɪn yourjʊr powerˈpaʊər. Whenwɛn youju singsɪŋ sadlyˈsædli Iaɪ crykraɪ; whenwɛn joyfullyˈʤɔɪfəli, Iaɪ laughlæf. Aə sadsæd melodyˈmɛlədi carriesˈkæriz memi backbæk totu Motherˈmʌðər Barberinˈbɑrbərɪn. Iaɪ seesi herhɜr inɪn ouraʊər househaʊs. Andænd Iaɪ don'tdoʊnt evenˈivən understandˌʌndərˈstænd theðə wordswɜrdz — youju singsɪŋ inɪn Italianɪˈtæljən!"

Iaɪ sawsɔ tearstɪrz inɪn hishɪz eyesaɪz. "Youju don'tdoʊnt saddenˈsædən memi. Youju remindrɪˈmaɪnd memi ofʌv mymaɪ youthjuθ, mymaɪ beautifulˈbjutəfəl pastpæst. Youju areɑr aə sensitiveˈsɛnsɪtɪv childʧaɪld. You'lljul makemeɪk peopleˈpipəl weepwip andænd they'llðeɪl applaudəˈplɔd youju. Justʤʌst waitweɪt…"

Suddenlyˈsʌdənli hehi stoppedstɑpt. Iaɪ sensedsɛnst hehi wantedˈwɑntɪd totu sayseɪ nonoʊ moremɔr. Onlyˈoʊnli muchmʌʧ laterˈleɪtər diddɪd Iaɪ understandˌʌndərˈstænd whywaɪ.

Hehi taughttɔt musicˈmjuzɪk theðə sameseɪm wayweɪ — woodenˈwʊdən tilestaɪlz carvedkɑrvd withwɪð notesnoʊts: trebleˈtrɛbəl clefklɛf onɑn onewʌn sidesaɪd, bassbeɪs clefklɛf onɑn theðə otherˈʌðər.

Musicˈmjuzɪk waswʌz nonoʊ easierˈiziər. Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs, sosoʊ patientˈpeɪʃənt withwɪð theðə dogsdɑgz, oftenˈɔfən lostlɔst hishɪz temperˈtɛmpər withwɪð memi.

"Withwɪð anən animalˈænəməl, onewʌn toleratesˈtɑləˌreɪts. Butbʌt youju — you'lljul bebi theðə deathdɛθ ofʌv memi!"

Iaɪ wantedˈwɑntɪd totu sayseɪ theðə monkeyˈmʌŋki mockedmɑkt theðə teacherˈtiʧər tootu, butbʌt respectrɪˈspɛkt — andænd fearfɪr — heldhɛld mymaɪ tonguetʌŋ.
