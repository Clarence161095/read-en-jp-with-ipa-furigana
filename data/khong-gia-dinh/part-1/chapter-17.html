Aə MASTERˈmæstər OFʌv STREETstrit CHILDRENˈʧɪldrən

Theðə deeperˈdipər wewi wentwɛnt intoˈɪntu Parisˈpærɪs, theðə furtherˈfɜrðər itɪt waswʌz fromfrʌm mymaɪ dreamsdrimz. Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs turnedtɜrnd intoˈɪntu aə poorpur districtˈdɪstrɪkt. Talltɔl blackblæk buildingsˈbɪldɪŋz crowdedˈkraʊdɪd togethertəˈgɛðər.

Iaɪ readrɛd theðə streetstrit nameneɪm: Lourcinelurˈsin. Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs askedæskt: "Isɪz Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli homehoʊm?" "Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli isɪz theðə masterˈmæstər Iaɪ toldtoʊld youju aboutəˈbaʊt. Hehi liveslɪvz herehɪr."

Wewi climbedklaɪmd fourfɔr flightsflaɪts ofʌv slipperyˈslɪpəri stairsstɛrz intoˈɪntu aə largelɑrʤ roomrum. Twelvetwɛlv bedsbɛdz linedlaɪnd theðə wallswɔlz. Theðə wallswɔlz werewɜr blackblæk withwɪð smokesmoʊk.

Aə boybɔɪ ofʌv tentɛn appearedəˈpɪrd — hugehjuʤ headhɛd, thinθɪn bodyˈbɑdi, patientˈpeɪʃənt eyesaɪz fullfʊl ofʌv sadnessˈsædnɪs. Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs toldtoʊld memi totu staysteɪ andænd leftlɛft. Theðə boybɔɪ askedæskt inɪn Italianɪˈtæljən: "Areɑr youju fromfrʌm mymaɪ countryˈkʌntri?"

"Nonoʊ, I'maɪm Frenchfrɛnʧ." Hehi saidsɛd: "Betterˈbɛtər forfɔr youju. Nonoʊ onewʌn sayssɛz 'luckyˈlʌki' totu anyoneˈɛniˌwʌn workingˈwɜrkɪŋ forfɔr Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli."

Hishɪz nameneɪm waswʌz Mattiaˈmɑtiə, fromfrʌm Luccaˈlukə. Hishɪz widowedˈwɪdoʊd motherˈmʌðər hadhæd sixsɪks childrenˈʧɪldrən. Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli waswʌz hishɪz uncleˈʌŋkəl.

Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli gavegeɪv Mattiaˈmɑtiə twotu whitewaɪt micemaɪs andænd setsɛt aə quotaˈkwoʊtə ofʌv thirtyˈθɜrti soussuz dailyˈdeɪli. Eachiʧ missingˈmɪsɪŋ sousu meantmɛnt aə beatingˈbitɪŋ.

Becausebɪˈkɔz Mattiaˈmɑtiə waswʌz uglyˈʌgli, peopleˈpipəl gavegeɪv himhɪm nothingˈnʌθɪŋ. Eachiʧ missingˈmɪsɪŋ sousu costkɑst onewʌn potatopəˈteɪtoʊ fromfrʌm dinnerˈdɪnər.

Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli keptkɛpt Mattiaˈmɑtiə homehoʊm totu cookkʊk soupsup. Butbʌt theðə potpɑt waswʌz lockedlɑkt withwɪð aə hingehɪnʤ andænd padlockˈpædˌlɑk. Mattiaˈmɑtiə couldkʊd onlyˈoʊnli smellsmɛl theðə soupsup throughθru aə tinyˈtaɪni pipepaɪp inɪn theðə lidlɪd.

Mattiaˈmɑtiə saidsɛd: "Iaɪ wantwɑnt totu bebi trulyˈtruli sicksɪk. Thenðɛn they'llðeɪl sendsɛnd memi totu theðə hospitalˈhɑspɪtəl. Theðə sistersˈsɪstərz speakspik gentlyˈʤɛntli thereðɛr."

Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli hadhæd struckstrʌk Mattia'sˈmɑtiəz headhɛd withwɪð aə stickstɪk. Aə largelɑrʤ bumpbʌmp hadhæd swelledswɛld. Hehi waswʌz inɪn terribleˈtɛrəbəl painpeɪn.

Atæt gatheringˈgæðərɪŋ timetaɪm, childrenˈʧɪldrən returnedrɪˈtɜrnd onewʌn bybaɪ onewʌn, carryingˈkæriɪŋ violinsˌvaɪəˈlɪnz, harpshɑrps, orɔr micemaɪs cagesˈkeɪʤɪz.

Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli arrivedəˈraɪvd — shortʃɔrt, gauntgɔnt, withwɪð aə chillingˈʧɪlɪŋ gazegeɪz. Twotu boysbɔɪz rushedrʌʃt totu taketeɪk hishɪz hathæt andænd bringbrɪŋ hishɪz chairʧɛr.

"Mymaɪ littleˈlɪtəl angelsˈeɪnʤəlz, timetaɪm totu countkaʊnt!" Childrenˈʧɪldrən shortʃɔrt ofʌv moneyˈmʌni werewɜr whippedwɪpt bybaɪ Riccardorɪˈkɑrdoʊ.

Fivefaɪv childrenˈʧɪldrən strippedstrɪpt theirðɛr shirtsʃɜrts. Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli turnedtɜrnd awayəˈweɪ, pretendingprɪˈtɛndɪŋ hehi couldkʊd notnɑt bearbɛr totu watchwɑʧ. "Eachiʧ screamskrim earnsɜrnz anən extraˈɛkstrə lashlæʃ," hehi warnedwɔrnd. Aə victimˈvɪktɪm criedkraɪd: "Motherˈmʌðər!"

Justʤʌst thenðɛn Vitalisvɪˈtɑlɪs burstbɜrst inɪn. Hehi snatchedsnæʧt theðə whipwɪp fromfrʌm Riccardorɪˈkɑrdoʊ andænd stoodstʊd talltɔl beforebɪˈfɔr Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli.

"Shamefulˈʃeɪmfəl! Tormentingtɔrˈmɛntɪŋ helplessˈhɛlplɪs childrenˈʧɪldrən! Iaɪ willwɪl reportrɪˈpɔrt youju totu theðə policepəˈlis!"

Garofoliˌgæroʊˈfoli threatenedˈθrɛtənd: "Reportrɪˈpɔrt memi? Iaɪ knownoʊ yourjʊr secretˈsikrɪt. Onewʌn nameneɪm isɪz allɔl Iaɪ neednid."
